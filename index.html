About Us

Your Trusted Partner in Quality Agricultural Products

TCM Farmer Producer Company

Established in 2019, TCM Farmer Producer Company has emerged as a leading manufacturer and exporter of premium agricultural products. Located in the fertile lands of Badal, Solan, Himachal Pradesh, we specialize in producing and supplying high-quality Garlic, Ginger, Raw Turmeric, Guava, and K3 Dry Leaf.

Under the expert leadership of Mr. Malkeet Singh, who brings 7 years of industry experience, our company has grown from strength to strength. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of product quality and packaging, ensuring that our customers receive nothing but the best.

With an annual turnover of 5-10 lakhs and a dedicated team of 2-3 employees, we pride ourselves on our quality certifications and numerous achievements in the agricultural sector. Our production capacity of 5 tons allows us to meet domestic demand efficiently while maintaining our commitment to excellence.
